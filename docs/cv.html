Parkuest

Founder 2012 - 2018

I developed and led the implementation of Parkuest, a comprehensive parking guidance system. My work included the design and development of ultrasonic sensor technology, network structure, and the integration of PIC family microcontrollers. I coordinated the manufacturing of PCBs in China and their assembly in Mexico. Additionally, I designed and developed the plastic enclosures for the devices, created a proprietary communication protocol, and designed custom displays for real-time data visualization. The system was successfully installed in the World Trade Center in Paraguay, as well as in locations in Sydney and Mexico.
